Spurthi Initiation..

SPURTHI TRUST is a pioneer with a novel idea to reach the people, who have not directly access to the information, education and Technology .The SPURTHI is full of good professionals and who are well experienced and knowledgeable Persons in Rural Development Sector. The team SPURTHI is with more clarity and ambitious to hold social responsibility to enhance the Livelihood opportunities and it will show the end result to the people directly. We have basic Transparency systems to funds flow and basic infrastructure facilities to reach the goals and Objectives of the organization

Aim and Objectives of society

To access the fundamental rights to the needy people through creating awareness about the Education, Health, and Sanitation, and usage of Natural resources

To grab the integrated livelihood opportunities from all the corners to provide Access to the target people of rural/urban/tribal in north costal area of Andrapradesh.

Mission

Therefore SPURTHI will take all necessary initiatives /measurer towards empowerment and self-determinations of community people enabling them to solve their own socio-economic problems by themselves towards poverty elevation.
