On going activities in 2020


Food Distribution for the needy people

COVID-19 a big disaster during 2020, the entire world is fighting with a small Virus. All the roads are silent, no shops, no hotels; no one is roaming in the outside. Everyone is staying at home for their safety. Police man is standing on the road to protect us, sanitary workers are cleaning the surrounds, and Doctors are treating the people who got corona virus in the Hospitals. SPURTHI team started to serve the food to labour, Beggars, orphans, old age people and patients in the hospitals in and around Vizianagaram urban area. SPRTHI distributed food for 8500 people till today and the same process is still going on in urban area.

In Rural, no labours work to do and no pay. They are unable to get money as well as the food. SPURTHI stood behind them and distributed vegetables to the below poverty line people. SPURTHI distributed vegetables Lady Fingers, Beans, Tomato, onions and dall. SPURTHI distributed vegetables for 1800 people in the villages, Gajapathinagar (4 SCs colonies and 1BC colony), Puritipenta SC colony, Thammaraidupeta, M.Gumadam, of Gajapathinagaram mandal and T.Burjavalasa, Timiteru, Sarayyavalasa, Datti, komatipalli Vindyavasi of Dattirajeru mandal till today and the same process is going on. For which funding provided by BAKTABALLA and FRIENDS from TANA (Telugu Association of North America)

Yoga classes to the children

During this period (COVID-19) entertaining the children is a big task to the parents. They have to attend to the online lessons, on par with online games. Many children are engaging into cell games. Due to nuclear families, no elders in the home to tell the stories. They should not go to out due to Corona virus. They have to spend lot of time within the home only. That will make them to irritate more. Thats why SPURTHI wants to divert them into this way and introduced YOGA classes for one hour daily within the campus. SPURTHI trained 120 children in Viziangaram urban. SPURTHI will introduce in the villages so soon after corona phase out.
