Aims and objects of the Society

To access the fundamental rights to the needy people through creating awareness about the Education, Health, and Sanitation, and usage of Natural resources

To grab the integrated livelihood opportunities from all the corners to provide Access to the target people of rural/urban/tribal in north costal area of Andrapradesh.

Objectives:

  • To bring the sustainable development, various lively hood activities are to be planned for the needy people.
  • To enhance the production and productivity in Agriculture and allied fields (Horti, Fishery, AH..etc) Technology and infrastructure facilities are to be taken place to minimize the expenditure to the target people.
  • To improve the quality of life, upgrading the skill and bring the accessibility of the all schemes are been with the Government and Non Government organizations.
  • To strengthen the Individuals/groups/Gender to overcome the risky stage, rapid trainings and capacity building programmes are to be planned as per the need of the target people.
  • To create a safety Zone for the Rural/Urban/Tribal people, Environment, Natural resources and Biodiversity can be protected by using the Government/Non Government/CSR/Corporate programmes and services
